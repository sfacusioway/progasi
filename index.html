Progasi Smart Max 150

The PROGASI Max 150 of the 2023 model year is a new sports and utilitarian lightweight enduro model designed specifically for multi-purpose use. PROGASI Max 150 is a choice for both advanced beginners and experienced riders. The powerful and reliable LF CG150 (162FMJ OHV) EVO-2 engine with a 5-speed gearbox paired with a Nibbi Racing 30 sports carburetor create the perfect tandem, providing maximum engine thrust at low and medium speeds, which is so necessary for driving on rough terrain and off-road.

Technical specifications

Type of motorcycle Light enduro
Engine Type 4-stroke, air-cooled, 2 valves per cylinder
Fuel supply system NIBBI RACING Carburetor
Transmission 5-speed 1-N-2-3-4-5
Battery PROGASI 12N4Ah
Engine start system Electro/Kickstarter
Motorcycle Dimensions (L/W/W) 1870×790×920
